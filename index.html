Jaegeon Lee

I am a research team administrator in the Department of Geography at Seoul National University (SNU). I received my master's degree in Feb. 2023 in Geography at SNU, advised by Prof. Jungyul Sohn.

My research interests center on the equity of human mobility mobility patterns in urban areas. During the master's program, I have conducted research projects on the differential change in mobility patterns among elders and commuters during the COVID-19 pandemic.

I aim to delve more deeply into the relationship between urban form and human mobility for further studies. I am currently researching on the way to represent residential environments from a bottom-up perspective based on point-of-interest data, which can serve as the basis of explaining daily mobility patterns at the disaggregated level.

  • We employed multilevel models to analyze the longitudinal changes in commuting flows (from mobile-based mobility data).

  • We demonstrated internal variations in which commuters from more educated residential areas experienced a more rapid decline, primarily due to the ease of adopting remote work among skilled workers.

Disparity in Destination Choices Among Elderly Travelers in Seoul

Which group of elderly individuals more readily changed their destination choices during the COVID-19 pandemic? We utilized cosine-similarity measures to compare the Origin-Destination (O-D) matrices (from smart-card data) before and after the COVID-19 pandemic. We suggested that elderly individuals with higher socioeconomic status and living closer to the urban center changed their destination choices to a greater extent.

Published in Journal of the Korean Regional Science Association with Prof. Jungyul Sohn.

Addressing the Cluttering Problem in Mobility Flow Visualization

How can we address the cluttering problem when visualizing mobility data at high spatial resolution? We improved the visualization of human mobility flows by applying the community detection method with a spatial-contiguity constraint.

Published in Journal of the Korean Regional Science Association with Prof. Gunhak Lee.
