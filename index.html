Jaegeon Lee

Hi, I am Jaegeon Lee, currently a research team administrator in the Department of Geography at Seoul National University. My research interest includes the travel patterns of elders and commuters, the travel disparity between socioeconomic classes, and the interaction between human mobility and urban forms. In my master's thesis, I researched how unevenly commuting patterns have changed during the COVID-19 pandemic period, advised by Prof. Jungyul Sohn.

I am currently working on the representation of spatial arrangements of consumer amenities, using POI data and neural network embedding methods, to gain an understanding of urban forms in a bottom-up perspective,
